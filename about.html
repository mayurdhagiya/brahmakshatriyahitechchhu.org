About Us

Connecting a community across generations

Brahmakshatriya Hitechchhu is more than a magazine — it is the shared voice of our community, carrying our heritage forward and celebrating the people who shape it.

Who We Are

A trust-led monthly publication dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of the Brahmakshatriya community, in print and online.

Our Mission

To inform, inspire and connect — sharing stories, achievements and traditions that keep the community informed and bonded across cities and generations.

Our Vision

A vibrant, well-networked community where every member feels seen, supported and proud of their roots — and where our youth carry the legacy forward with confidence.

What We Offer

  • Monthly editions covering community news
  • Interviews with leaders and changemakers
  • Cultural insights and historical features
  • A platform for member achievements

Join Us

Whether as a reader, contributor or supporter, you are welcome in our journey. Get in touch to subscribe or contribute a story.

Our Heritage

Published continuously since 1976, the magazine has chronicled decades of community life — celebrations, milestones, and the people who built our institutions.
